Why Traditional Costs More

لماذا الطريقة التقليدية أغلى؟

  • Uniform grid placement ignores load centres → longer cables
  • Over-provisioned transformers (60% rule) → unnecessary units
  • No road access planning → higher maintenance callout costs
⚡

What Our Model Adds

ما الذي يضيفه نموذجنا؟

  • Weighted K-Means reduces cable by ~23–40%
  • 80% loading rule → fewer, right-sized transformers
  • Road-snapped placement → faster maintenance, lower annual cost

Enclosure Design Options

Each station adopts a design language suited to its surrounding zone context.

Stone-Clad Enclosure
Natural or engineered stone finish with concealed ventilation louvers. Ideal near roundabouts, fuel stations, and commercial strips. Dimensions: 3100 × 2300 mm.
Urban Commercial
Natural Rock Form
Industrial shell shaped as a large boulder. Hidden side air inlets and concealed top hot-air outlets. Zero visual impact in parks and open spaces. Width: 4.0–5.0 m.
Parks & Roundabouts
Najdi Traditional Architecture
Earthen plaster finish with triangular ventilation openings and curved parapets. Seamless integration with Saudi heritage neighbourhoods. Dimensions: 2600 × 2800 × 3200 mm.
Heritage Districts
Classical Heritage Style
Limestone facade with arched mashrabiya grilles, ornamental cornices, and heritage lanterns. Ideal for upscale residential and premium districts. Dimensions: 2800 × 2200 × 3000 mm.
Residential Premium
Modern Minimalist
Concrete panel with vertical aluminium louvres, integrated accent lighting, and planter base. Suited to contemporary mixed-use and commercial zones.

Islamic Geometric Screen
